Turner “Tfue” Tenney is a content creator and streamer who rose to fame as a professional Fortnite player. He was one of the most popular and watched Fortnite players and had a very large following after diversifying and focusing on games other than Fortnite.

Tfue has played for Rogue, Denial Esports, and FaZe Clan. With FaZe he got into a legal dispute about his contract and subsequently left the organization.
